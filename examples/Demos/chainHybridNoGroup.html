chainHybridNoGroup.scn

Description:

Scene describing the interactions between several objects with different internal behavior:
  • Finite Element Method using Tetrahedra (red object)
  • Mass-Springs (green object)
  • Free Form Deformation (Yellow object)
  • Rigid (gray object)
Interesting Point:

The collision is handled independently for each object by separate solvers, which lead to instabilities, and the explosion of the scene.
See Also:

chainHybrid for stability improvement, with the addition of a CollisionGroupManager component.
